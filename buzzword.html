BuzzWord

When non-native english speakers get their dream jobs in the US, it is a challenging process to learn all the necessary vocabulary and terminology from the industry to succeed in the day to day.

BuzzWord will help non-native English professionals to effectively communicate at the workplace. With a simple and easy learning interface, a daily “buzzword” is a stress free way to brush up language skills!

Research

Research Question

Discover participants’ pain points while communicating at the workplace as non-native English speakers.

This study is the first step to designing an app that supports non-native English professionals to communicate effectively at the workplace.

Our Goals

Learn about the communication challenges of working in the US as a non-native speaker.

Discover the different tools participants use to overcome language barriers at work.

Discover the importance of knowing the industry vocabulary.

Insights

Non-native English speakers in a professional setting feel pressure to be updated on idioms and industry terms.

Non-native English speakers are often worried about using incorrect grammar, mispronouncing words, or saying the wrong thing at work.

Non-native English speakers need a quick way to brush up on specific language to excel in professional and social settings.

Problem statement

Non-native English speakers do not have a resource to quickly learn niche and technical language that can be used in professional and social settings.

Value Proposition

Given one word a day, buzzword is a stress-free platform that helps non-english native speakers to quickly expand their vocabulary and effectively communicate in professional and social settings.
